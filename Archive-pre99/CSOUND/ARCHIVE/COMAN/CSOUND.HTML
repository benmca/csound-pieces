Acoustic Guitar
PhM / CWS - SDL model Josep M Comajuncosas
A fully MIDIfied Acoustic Guitar based on papers from Tero Tolonen. The body impulse response comes from a Ramirez guitar and was kindly provided by Dr. Julius Orion Smith III. 
The sound is maybe too dull. Experiment with the user definable parameters! 
Synthesis technique: Commuted Waveguide Synthesis (2 Single Delay Loop strings in parallel) 
This instrument is polyphonic. Pitchbend works but can cause some discontinuities if it is too drastic. Maybe I´ll implement a transient eliminator routine later on. Notice the fractional delay filter is a simple 1st order Lagrange interpolator by now. Suggested sr = 44100 otherwise you must resample the IR. kr can be lower. 
Get guitar.zip (includes orc&sco files, a recorded guitar IR and a MIDI file) mp3 sample

Clarinet
PhM / Dynamic System - SDL model Josep M Comajuncosas
A fairly sophisticated set of waveguide clarinets based on the paper "Physical Models of Wind Instruments: A Generalized Excitation Coupled with a Modular Tube Simulation Platform" by Gijs de Bruin and Maarten von Walstijn (JNMR Vol.24 pp 148-163). 
The excitation model derived by Keefe even includes air inertance and soft clipping for reed beating, and is solved in discrete time using a specially optimized integration scheme. 
Maarten was kind enough helping me in this Csound implementation, adding some improvements specially in the reflectance filter, the dual bore system for note transitions and a new soft clipping routine. 
Notice kr must equal sr = 44100 in this implementation. It runs in 2 instruments, instr1 is the MIDIfied one, and instr2 must be left activated during the performance. 
COMING SOON mp3 sample
 
 
 
  
Wts drone
WTS Josep M Comajuncosas
An example of Wave Terrain Synthesis from my HTML chapter to be included in R. Boulanger´s Csound book. It generates a virtual surface by creating a rather high number of f-tables.Once the tables have been stored in memory, it is efficient to index the "surface" with multiple and slightly different orbits, for example placing the center of the orbit at different places in the surface. This orquestra creates in this way a very dense, deep analog-like drone sound. The mp3 example has been compressed and reenveloped after rendering.
Get wts_drone.zip mp3 sample
  
 
 
Metallic squeal
PhM ? Josep M Comajuncosas
This instrument can generate massive amounts of feedback noise by chaining a repluck unit with an IIR comb filter whose clipped output is in turn sent back to the repluck opcode, as if a guitar pickup was excited by the sound coming from the speaker. It emulates quite convincingly the guitar my neighbour uses to play at saturday evenings...
Get fdbk.zip mp3 sample
 

 
Techno bass
NLDS Josep M Comajuncosas
Synthesis by Functional Iterations. This instrument is based on an article by Di Scipio & Prignano, Journal of New Music Research, vol.25 (1996), pp. 31-46. 
Basically with these settings it creates an analog-like bass whose harmonic richness and eventually noise content can be adjustable by approaching the chaotic region of the bifurcation map.
Get siniter.zip mp3 sample

TB 303 bass line
Analog Modelling Josep M Comajuncosas
A rather complex instrument which tries to emulate the classic Roland TB303 bassline. It includes bandlimited sawtooth waves, 24dB/oct Resonant Lowpass filter, ftable-based sequencer with accent & slide and lots of minutiae to get that wonderful acid sound. To be included in R. Boulanger´s Csound book together with some other analog emulations.
Get 303emu.zip mp3 sample

Analog Vocoder
Analog Modelling Josep M Comajuncosas
My very own 8-band routable vocoder, employing a Solina string ensemble as the carrier and a drum pattern as the modulator. Flexible routing with the zak system and selectable frequency with table look-up. Improved freq. response with the addition of an unvocoded hi.freq. signal. Judge the results ! (the mp3 sample has been compressed at 1:2 after rendering). note: To use it you may create a sound file to be called as the modulator input, by default named "f:\drums.wav".
Get jcnvoc.zip mp3 sample
