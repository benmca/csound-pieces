midi-message [Class]

A high-level representation of a raw MIDI message.

Slots declared by midi-message:

message {integer}
The raw midi message, represented as a formatted 28 bit fixnum. See MIDI Messages for more infomation on MIDI messages.
data {list}
A list of any additional data bytes.
Additional slots are inherited from note.
