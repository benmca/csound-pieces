sequence [Pattern]

Enumerate items in serial order. Once the last item is reached it is continually reselected by the pattern.

Example:

(setf x (items a b c d e in sequence))
#<SEQUENTIAL-ITEM-STREAM 136377321>

? (read-items x 10)
(A B C D E E E E E E)
