hide {objects} [Command]

Makes {objects} invisible to normal musical event processing. A hidden object does not appear in output but still occupies a position in its container, and is shown in listings marked "hidden". It is more efficient to hide a container than all its subobjects. Use the UNHIDE command to unhide hidden objects.
