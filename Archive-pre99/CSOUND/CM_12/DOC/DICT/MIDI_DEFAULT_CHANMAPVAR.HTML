*midi-default-channel-map* [Variable]

A sequence of mappings from logical channels to channels and routes. Mappings must be of the form

(logical-channel (oute channel))
As with the route map, mappings may be many-to-many. The default value maps 16 logical channels (numbered 0-15) to route 0, channels 0-15.

Example:

? (setf *midi-default-channel-map*
    #((0 (0 0)) (0 (0 1)) (0 (0 2)) (0 (0 3))
      (1 (0 1))
      (2 (0 2))
      ...))

See Also:

*midi-default-route-map*, midi-set-channel-map
