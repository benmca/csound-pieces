transpose {objects} {slot expr}+ [Command]

Maps over {objects} and transposes each slot in {slot expr}+ by its corresponding expr. transpose is only meaningful for slots containing scale or frequency data.

Example:

Transpose the freq slot of all objects in foo by -6 degrees and the freq2 slot by between 0 and 4 degrees: 
Stella [Top-Level]: transpose foo freq -6 freq2 (between 0 4)

See Also:

increment, invert, map, scale, set, unset
