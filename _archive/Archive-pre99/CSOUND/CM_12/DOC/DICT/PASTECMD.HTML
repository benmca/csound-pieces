paste {place}* [Command]

Copies the current contents of the pasteboard to {place}. If {place} is not specified a new container is created to hold the pasted material. Otherwise, {place} can be the postion or range in an existing container or the name of a new container to receive the pasted material.

Example:

Copy the pasteboard's material to a new object Foo: 
Stella [Top-Level]: paste foo
Paste to a subrange of Foo: 
Stella [Top-Level]: paste foo[5:10]

See Also:

copy, cut, duplicate
