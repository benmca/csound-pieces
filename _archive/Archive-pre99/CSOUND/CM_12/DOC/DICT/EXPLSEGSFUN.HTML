explsegs length sum power &key :return-type [Function]

Like explseg but returns all length number of segments in a list.

Example:

? (setf l (explsegs 4 10 2))
(1.8920711500272102 2.250064473703739 2.6757926813433408
  3.18207169492571)

? (apply #'+ l)
10.0

See Also:

expl, explseg
