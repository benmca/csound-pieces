invert {objects} {slot expr}+ [Command]

Maps over {objects} and inverts each slot in {slot expr}+ around its corresponding expr. invertis only meaningful for slots containing scale or frequency data.

Example:

To invert the note slot of all objects in foo around C4: 
Stella [Top-Level]: invert foo note c4

See Also:

increment, map, scale, set, transpose unset
