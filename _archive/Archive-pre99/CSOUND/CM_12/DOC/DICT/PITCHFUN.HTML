pitch reference &optional scale [Function]

Returns the floating point frequency of reference in scale. Reference may be a note, degree, or pitch. If scale is omitted it defaults to *standard-scale*.

Example:

? (pitch 'a4)
440.0

? (pitch 69)
440.0

? (pitch 440.0)
440.0
